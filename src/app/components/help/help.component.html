Help & Support

Hazard Report

This application simplifies hazard reporting process. It offers accuracy as hazard location, picture, date and time are captured automatically when the report is made and sent.

Hassles of writing report on a paper form or card are eliminated. No more false or fake reports are made and submitted.


Private Mode

Set to private mode, this application will work exclusively for your organization or safety department only. Published reports are not exposed to users outside your organization. Only employees or members of organization can submit and view reports on this mode.

To enable private mode, you need organization ID that contains combination of uppercase/lowercase alphabets and numbers. Please contact us if you want to implement this application inside your organization and get an organization ID.


Safety Admin Application

We also offer a web application for you to list all submitted hazard reports, follow up actions and closing them as well as well as generating reports for review and safety improvement purposes.


Public & Community Usage

This app is also useful for community and public as any nearby identified hazards reported will be visible to all users. Hazard awareness can be achieved and keeping all parties away from potential dangers even disasters. People are encouraged to report any hazards at workplace and any environment to help others.

Public hazards are categorized into 3 different distance ranges. Nearby (2 kms), Far(10 kms) and Further(30 kms). Please contact us to suggest of widening the range.
